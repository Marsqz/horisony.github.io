AI Shopping Assistant
AI Shopping Assistant is an intelligent platform that helps people do online shopping in a more intuitive and efficient way. Users can chat with the AI shopping bot to buy what they want, either from eBay, Amazon, Walmart or any other ecommerce websites.
Background
"AI Shopping Assistant" is an innovation project selected for top 50 projects for eBay Skunkworks Expo in San Jose in 2016. A team of 3 including me turned this project from idea to prototype, with me responsible for interaction and visual design. Below are the team members.
Problems of Traditional Online Shopping
1. Too many accounts to be managed.
2. Hard to compare products from different website.
3. The shopping app or website can't understand customers well.
Solution
What if people can buy what they want from all different websites by one app.
Use Case
Concept1 -- Chatbot
Get what you want by chatting with an AI shopping assistant.
