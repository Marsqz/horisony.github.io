eBay Journey 2016
eBay Journey 2016 is a website that shows a customer what he or she has done on eBay in 2016. It displays the user's GMB, GMV, first purchasing, first selling, etc., all in real data supported by eBay Data Service and Solutions (DSS) Department.
Goal
The goal is to remind eBay users of their special moments on eBay, try to get them back to eBay in the coming future.
