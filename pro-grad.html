Graduation Project
My project is 'Breathe', focusing on improving the quality of air that people are breathing. This project develops a product that purify the air, just like a “lung” on your window that helps your room breathe. Here is a book of my project.
Problems
As air quality deteriorates with the development of manufacturing industry in China, an increasing number of people are getting respiratory diseases. Breathing polluted air every day damages people’s health, particularly to vulnerable people such as pregnant women, newborn baby, patients and the old. Therefore, it’s signicant to desigh a product to purify the air.
