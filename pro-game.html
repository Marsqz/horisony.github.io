We Are The Customer
"We Are The Customer" (WATC) is a website where eBay emplyees can compete for buying, selling on eBay, or visiting eBay. I am responsible for some new features' design like 'What's good', 'Duel Recommendation' and 'Date Indication'.
1. What's Good
After holiday season, many people must have purchased a lot of items on eBay, why not share them with others.
We provide a platform where every eBay employee can share what they've bought on eBay, other people can view and vote by thumb up, or go to the item's link on eBay and buy it.
To make it noticed by users, I made it animated.
2. Duel Recommendation
To make it easier for people to find someone to duel, we added the 'Duel Recommendation' module, where 5 people will be shown on top of duel page, and users can duel them as quickly as clicking a button. This greatly increased duel usage.
3. Competition Date Indication
Even with date range of competitions below competition title, users couldn't get an intuitive sense about how long will this competition start or end.
We decided to add date labels on top of banners to show how many days will this competition start or end, arousing users' emtions like anticipation or sense of urgency.
